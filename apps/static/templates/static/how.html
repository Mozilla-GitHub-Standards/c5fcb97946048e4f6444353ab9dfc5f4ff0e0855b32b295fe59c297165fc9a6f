How It Works

Sep 11-18:

The competition is open! Submit your art as a Persona design here. How do you create a Persona design? Follow this easy-to-use tutorial.

Sep 22 - Oct 6:

Community voting period! The 10 Persona designs with the most active daily users (ADUs) at the end of the competition advance to the finals.

What is an active daily user (ADU)? An active daily user is a person that selects and keeps your Persona design in their browser. Once a day, we count the number of people that have selected and kept your design in their browser for more than 24 hours.

Invite your friends, family, and fans to download your Persona!

Oct 12-16:

Celebrity judges review the top 10 Personas.

Oct 21:

Top 3 Winners are announced and awarded prizes.
