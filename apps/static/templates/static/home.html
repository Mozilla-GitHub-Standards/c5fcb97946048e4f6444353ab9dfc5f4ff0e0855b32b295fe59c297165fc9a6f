Game On 2010 is the first ever international gaming competition organized by the folks at the Mozilla Labs Gaming project. Game On is all about games built, delivered and played on the Open Web and the browser. We want to explore the wider set of technologies which make immersive gaming on the Open Web possible. Whether you’re a student, indie game developer, or studio, you’re welcome to participate in creating awesome games for the Open Web. We have tons of fun surprises in store between now and 1/11/11 when the contest wraps.

So, stay in touch on the Mozilla Labs Gaming blog and on Twitter for all the details!
